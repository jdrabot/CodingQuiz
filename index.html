Coding Quiz Challenge

In this Coding Quiz Challenge, you are asked a series of five questions in a span of sixty seconds. If you get one correct, you will move on to the next question. If answered incorrect, you will proceed with a penalty of losing fifteen seconds. At the end of the quiz, your score will be determained by the remaining time. You can then enter your new score by inputting your intials. Press Start to begin.
